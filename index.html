Claude Project — Pressure Test

The Prompt That Challenged the Map

📤 Prompt Sent to Claude

I'm building a personal portfolio. My proof statement is:

"I help early-stage founders go from messy idea to a launched product — through design, code, and clarity — in weeks, not months."

My one action is: Book a Free Discovery Call.

My proposed sitemap has exactly 4 pages: Home/Hero → Work/Case Studies → About → Contact/Book.

Please pressure-test this sitemap against my proof statement and my one action. Tell me:

  1. Does every page directly support moving the visitor toward booking a call?
  2. Is there anything missing that could cause a drop-off?
  3. Is there anything present that doesn't earn its place?
  4. What is the one thing I should change or add?
🤖 Claude's Response (Summary)

✅ What Works: The 4-page flow is tight. Every page has a clear job and the single CTA direction (book a call) is consistent. No page contradicts the claim.

⚠️ Potential Drop-off Risk: Visitors who arrive on the Work page directly (via search or share) may not have seen the Hero promise. Each case study should open with a one-line reminder of who you help and why.

🚫 What Doesn't Earn Its Place: If your Contact page has a long form (more than 3 fields), it creates friction. The one action should feel like a single tap, not an interview.

💡 One Thing to Change: Add a micro-testimonial strip between the Hero and Work sections — a single line quote from a past client anchors credibility before the visitor even reaches the case studies. This doesn't require a new page; it's a section within Home.

📌
Change I'll Make (Noted from Pressure Test)
Add a 1–2 line micro-testimonial strip on the Home page between the Hero headline and the Work section. No new page — just a trust anchor inline. Also trim the Contact form to max 3 fields: Name, Email, "What's your product idea?" (one sentence).
Claude Project Setup

How to Configure Your Claude Project

1
Create the Project
Go to claude.ai → Click "Projects" → "New Project". Name it: "[Your Name] Portfolio Build — 8 Weeks"
2
Paste Custom Instructions
In "Project Instructions" paste your proof statement, your one action, and this line: "Act as my tutor and strategic thinking partner for this 8-week portfolio build."
3
Run the Pressure Test
Copy the prompt from the section above and paste it as your first message inside the new project. Save the response.
4
Note One Change
From Claude's answer, write down at least one thing you'll revise in your sitemap. This shows active thinking, not passive reading.
